About

This gallery showcases different animals shown on Earth; the Dog, the Cat, the Crow, the Deer, and the Shark. When clicking to view deatils on one of them, a paragraph describing the animal will be shown, and a to go back to the gallery at the bottom. Dog: A dog is a domesticated mammal known for its loyalty, intelligence, and companionship. It belongs to the canine family and comes in various breeds, sizes, and temperaments. Cat: A cat is a small, domesticated mammal known for its agility, independence, and playful behavior. It is a popular pet with sharp claws, keen senses, and a strong hunting instinct. Crow: A crow is a highly intelligent bird known for its black plumage, sharp beak, and distinctive cawing sound. It is part of the corvid family and is often associated with mystery and folklore. Deer: A deer is a graceful, herbivorous mammal known for its antlers and slender legs. It is often found in forests and grasslands, and is known for its agility and speed. Shark: A shark is a large, carnivorous fish known for its sizable, slick body, sharp teeth, and keen senses. Most sharks are great swimmers and top predators of marine life. Contrary to popular belief, they do not hunt humans.
